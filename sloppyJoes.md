# Sloppy Joe

## Ingredients

* 2 lbs, 90% ground beef
* 2/3 cup, chopped onion
* dash, pepper

* 1 1/3 cup, catsup
* 2/3 TB, mustard
* 2/3 TB, brown sugar
* 1/3 tsp, salt
* 1 1/3, cup water

## Cooking

1. Brown ground beef in a dutch oven with onion, pepper.
2. Add all other ingredients (catsup, mustard, brown sugar, salt, water).
3. Simmer for ~30 minutes. 

Serve on buns.
